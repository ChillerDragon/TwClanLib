How does it work:
Your Total Points is a SUMM of Fixed Points and Season Points.

How can you earn Fixed Points:
- When you finish the map FIRST time you earn Fixed Points.
- Fixed points are always the same and they never reduce!
How can you earn Season Points:
- When you finish the map AGAIN you earn Season Points.
- You can earn Season Points in Tournaments and Events
- Events are running every day on a random map
- Each day you LOOSE 5% of Season Points (not less then 1), but you can't get below 0.
